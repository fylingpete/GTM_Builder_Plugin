# High Expectation Customer™ (HXC™) Persona

> **Status:** empty_template — drafted during Step 1c and version-locked at the end of Step 3
> **Team-required:** no (solo-OK, optional team input)
> **Source:** Rahul Vohra's PMF Engine methodology, borrowing the HXC framework from Julie Supan

> **STRICT SOURCE RULE.** This persona must be extracted EXCLUSIVELY from
> Very Disappointed respondents' Q2 (who benefits most) and Q3 (main
> benefit) answers. Founder imagination is not allowed. Marketing
> personas that existed before the survey are not allowed. Interviews
> outside the survey pool are not allowed. If a trait appears in the
> persona that cannot be traced to a specific Very Disappointed response
> ID, remove it.

## How to use this file

The HXC is the composite portrait of the user who is already falling in
love with the product. It is built from the Very Disappointed segment
because those are the only respondents who actually feel the core value;
drawing from anyone else dilutes the picture with people who do not feel
it. The persona has a concrete name, a role, a work context, a main
benefit felt, and a frustration or desire. Every non-obvious claim in
the persona is annotated with the response IDs that support it, so the
trace back to raw data is always available.

The persona is drafted as `v1-draft` during Step 1c. At the end of Step
3 it is promoted to `v1-locked` so the first Quarterly Rebuild has a
stable reference point. Subsequent Quarterly Rebuilds may produce `v2`,
`v3`, etc. — each new version is added to the Version History section
below rather than overwriting the prior one.

## Rules (from the substance rubric)

- **hxc_from_very_disappointed_only (non-negotiable):** persona inputs
  come exclusively from Q2 and Q3 answers of Very Disappointed respondents.
- **has_role_context:** persona records a concrete role or title and a
  work context.
- **has_main_benefit:** main benefit felt is present, derived from Q3.
- **has_frustration_or_desire:** at least one frustration or desire is
  present.
- **hxc_has_name (recommendation):** persona is given a concrete,
  original first name (or first + last). Do not reuse example names from
  reference material.
- **hxc_version_locked (Step 3 checklist + recommendation):** persona
  carries an explicit version marker at the end of Step 3.
