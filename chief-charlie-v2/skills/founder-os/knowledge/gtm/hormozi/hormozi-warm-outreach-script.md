## Script: The Warm Outreach Offer — Alex Hormozi

After building rapport with ACA (Acknowledge, Compliment, Ask), transition into the offer using this structure.

### The Script Structure

1. **Describe their struggles** — Show you understand their situation. "I know a lot of people who [specific pain point they mentioned]..."

2. **Paint the dream outcome** — Describe what life looks like on the other side. Be specific: "Imagine if you could [desired result] within [timeframe]"

3. **Create scarcity** — "I'm taking on 5 case studies right now" — a small number makes it exclusive and real. You genuinely can only serve a few people while building your skills

4. **Give a reason why** — Why the discount, why them, why now. "I'm building my portfolio" or "I'm testing a new approach and need feedback." Authenticity matters — don't fabricate urgency

5. **Add social proof** — Any results you have, even from free clients. "I helped someone do X and they got Y result"

6. **Include a guarantee** — Remove risk. "If you don't see results in 30 days, I'll refund you / keep working for free"

### The Referral Hinge

After delivering the offer, use the three-way text introduction: ask the person to introduce you to someone else via text message. This is warmer than cold outreach and carries implicit endorsement.

### Benchmarks

100 reach-outs per day. Expect a 20% reply rate. Of those who reply, 30-50% will become engaged leads. That's 3-5 engaged leads per day — enough to build a business from zero.
