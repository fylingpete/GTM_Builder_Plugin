## Script: Your First Paid Sale — Alex Hormozi

Making the leap from free to paid is terrifying. This script removes the friction by combining deep discounting with conditions that build your business.

### The Script

> "Since I'm only taking on five people for this, I'm offering it at 80% off. All I ask is that you promise to use it, give me honest feedback, and leave a review when you're done."

### Why This Works

**Scarcity** — "Only five people" creates urgency without feeling manipulative. It is true because you genuinely can only handle a few clients while learning.

**Deep discount** — 80% off makes the price a non-issue. The real currency you are collecting is proof: testimonials, case studies, and feedback.

**Three conditions** frame the discount as a fair exchange:
1. **Use it** — ensures they actually engage (dead customers teach you nothing)
2. **Give feedback** — turns every client into a product consultant
3. **Leave a review** — builds the social proof you need for full-price sales

### Price Escalation Strategy

After every five sales, raise the price by 20%. This creates a natural progression: your fifth customer pays more than your first, your tenth pays more than your fifth. One gym business started at $6,000 and gradually escalated past $50,000 using this exact method.

The discount is not a loss — it is an investment. You are buying proof and data at a fraction of what they are worth. Five glowing testimonials at 80% off are worth more than one reluctant sale at full price.

## Key Takeaway
Your first sales script should trade a steep discount for usage, feedback, and reviews — then raise prices 20% after every five sales.

## When to Apply
- You have never made a paid sale and don't know how to start
- You need testimonials but have no paying customers yet
- You are afraid to charge because you don't feel "ready"
